21. YOU HAVE OVERCOME ALREADY

Ye are of God, little children, and have overcome them: because greater is he that is in you, than he that is in the world. (1 John 4:4)

Our victory is not lingering in the future; we are victorious already.

Did you notice that this scripture does not give an assurance that we will overcome someday; rather, our victorious status is clearly portrayed. We have overcome already.

When I first read this scripture, I capitalized on the last sentence much more than I capitalized on the first. I was too quick to be excited over the fact that the greater one is in me.

Thus, I began to make a confession that since the greater one is in me, I will surely overcome. It took me further meditation on all the lines to realize that I have overcome already.

Our victory is not lingering in the future; no! It was with us, and is still with us. We are victorious already.

I always thought that David defeated Goliath in the battle field — I was wrong! Goliath was defeated long ago. What David did was just a physical manifestation of the victory that had since been attained.

At the war-front, it did not matter what David would have done to him; he was already a dead man. At that point, there was no need for any weapon or ammunition; just a slap would have brought him down.

One thing I admire about David is his confidence; he was so sure that he had overcome already, and soon, he got the world to believe that too. Likewise beloved, be confident in the truth that you have overcome already.

Quit praying that God should help you to overcome. David never did that. He was certain that he had overcome already. God will not help you to overcome; He has already done all the He will do. You just need to be confident in your victory; have faith!

The truth is, as Christians, most of the battles we are confronted with are won already. We overcame these battles when Jesus died and rose again!

When Jesus overcame death by His glorious resurrection, we also obtained victory over every ill situation. You have got the victory to that battle already.

The only edge the enemy can have over a Christian is ignorance; if you have overcome a battle without knowing it, you will never be able to assume the position of a champ.

Be confident in the truth that you have already overcome all of life’s’ battles. Stop worrying over the victory, it is certainly yours’. Be blessed!
