2. THE LORD DELIGHTS IN YOU

You shall no more be termed Forsaken, and your land shall no more be termed Desolate, but you shall be called My Delight Is in Her, and your land Married; for the LORD delights in you, and your land shall be married. (Isaiah 62:4 ESV)

Thinking of you, His heart is gladdened; looking at you, he wears a smile. You are the best of God’s creation.

Have you ever delighted in someone or something? A mere thought of it gladdens your heart, and dresses your face with a warm smile. That’s just the way the Lord delights in you, and continually has you in his Hand.

There are situations we may have gone that made us really feel forsaken; at such seasons, people asked, “where is your God?” Beloved, the season for entertaining such wrong thoughts that you’ve been forsaken is over!

We are inscribed on the palm of God, our walls are continually before Him; His eyes are fixed on you constantly, and He never gets tired of seeing you. His heart is gladdened, and He is delighted in you.

Reading a bit further, it is said that as the bridegroom rejoices over the bride, so does God rejoice over us.

Verily, if only you had an idea how the Lord delights and rejoices over you, you will not harbor any contradicting thought. Thinking of you, His heart is gladdened; looking at you, he wears a smile.

Much more than our affairs matter to us, it matters to God. If God provides for the birds of the air that doesn’t work; if He glamorously clothes the lilies and constantly watches over the sparrow; how much more you? It is written:

There is a river whose streams make glad the city of God, the holy place where the Most High dwells. (Psalms 46:4 NIV)

Beloved, you are that river. Streams of living waters gush out from your belly; and those streams gladden the heart of God. You are the best of God’s creation.

The Lord is proud of you. He never created any other thing to rule dominate and take charge, but man; He never made any other creature in his image and likeness, but man. For the mistake of man, God sacrificed His only son.

Verily, God has given us so much to live above everything; the only reason why you might not be living that great life, is because you have not yet stretched your hands to receive what God holds for you.

The fact that there are so many men with un-kept hairs on the street doesn’t mean that there is no barber on that same street; it simply means that they have not bothered to go for a shave.

Beloved, God loves you much more than you can imagine; He spared you from tragedy so many times; He constantly delights in you; only take refuge in His arms today.

Receive Him, and let him make life worth living; Be blessed!
