13. I DECLARE

This book of the law shall not depart out of thy mouth; but thou shalt meditate therein day and night, that thou mayest observe to do according to all that is written therein: for then thou shalt make thy way prosperous, and then thou shalt have good success. (Joshua 1:8)

The constant meditation and declaration of the Word of God, is what guides and ushers a man into higher realms that men have never soared on before.

Over recent years, the world has been shaken by various circumstances that drained a few people of the very little, which they had. Many are left stranded, in a pressing situation.

The economy has battled inflation, recession, deflation, amongst others. There has been a shift in the lives of many individuals. Thus, it is not quite predictable, what the nearest future may be like, if this persists.

The dire need and desire of many men have become prosperity and good success. Men are not just satisfied with success, but good success; a type of prosperity that remains ever green, even when the economy lacks stability... a type of success that does not fade over time. Real prosperity!

Only few get to realize that prosperity and good success is only found in God. I have seen many men that touched the peak of prosperity, and within a split second, it all faded. This has pushed them to a conclusion that wealth has got wings; they fly out just in the same manner as they flew in.

Beloved, Jesus said something significant to the Samaritan woman: “Jesus answered and said unto her, Whosoever drinketh of this water shall thirst again:

But whosoever drinketh of the water that I shall give him shall never thirst; but the water that I shall give him shall be in him a well of water springing up into everlasting life.” (John 4:13-14)

Verily, there is a kind of water that satisfies a man temporarily, but leaves him thirsty in no time; likewise, there is another kind of water (the living water) that permanently quenches all thirst and creates an innate fountain.

Beloved, just like the comparison of normal water to living water, we also can compare earthly success with Divine prosperity. Earthly success satisfies a man temporarily, but leaves him stranded within a short period, while, Divine prosperity creates a fountain of success within a man, which eternally expels poverty from him.

Well, based on our Word for today, the secret to Divine prosperity and good success emanates from constant meditation and declaration of the Word of God. This is what ushers a man into a realm of Divine success... a success that cannot be shaken by the height of economic crises.

Notice, it is never said, “This book of law shall not depart out of thy eyes”, but mouth. In essence, we should not just see and read the bible like a novel, but we should constantly speak Rhema out of it.

The constant meditation and declaration of the Word of God, is what guides and ushers a man into higher realms that men have never soared on before.

Let the Words be in our mouth always! The secret and mysteries of real greatness on earth is declaring Rhema.

Verily, there is no situation, for which Rhema is declared, that leaves you stranded or disappointed. When you need a job, appointment, or anything else, speak Rhema day and night. Declare it in the atmosphere at all seasons.

Beloved, above all things always bear in mind that you can always speak yourself in and out of any situation. Declare it!
