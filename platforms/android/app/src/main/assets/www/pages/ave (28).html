28. HE FUNCTIONS IN ME

If a man preaches, let him do it with God speaking through him. If a man helps others, let him do it with the strength God gives. So in all things God may be honored through Jesus Christ. Shining-greatness and power belong to Him forever. Let it be so. (1 Peter 4:11 NLV)

Until you grasp the understanding that you are not the one in action, but God, your results will always be limited.

If there’s one confession I can never cease to make, it is, “The Holy Spirit functions in me.” Beloved, this is solely the mindset, which I received, that turned my life all around for good.

By having a mindset that the Holy Spirit is resident and functions in you, you will understand that when you are speaking, it is God speaking through you (Your words can never fail); when you are involved in something, God is involved in it (It is a success); When you lay hands on the sick, it is God touching them (healing is certain); When you write an exam, God has just written through you (You’d only produce God’s result — the best).

Until you grasp the understanding that you are not the one in action, but God, your results will always be limited.

There is nothing as powerful as having the Holy Spirit function in you. You cannot be sick! Light and darkness have no union whatsoever; thus, the Holy Spirit cannot share boundaries with sickness.

Beloved there is nothing we cannot do when the Holy Spirit functions in us. Little wonder it is written: I can do all things through Christ which strengtheneth me. (Philippians 4:13).

Have this mindset always—the Holy Spirit functions in us; there is nothing that is impossible for us; we only produce God’s result! — But most importantly we should always attribute all glory to God.

Let God be honored in your success! When you have operated with this mindset, and all doors have swung open unto you, do not call it a mere coincidence. As it is written, ‘So in all things God may be honored through Jesus Christ. Shining-greatness and power belong to Him forever. Let it be so.’ Testify to the glory of God.

He functions in us, we can do all things through Christ; He will never share his glory with anyone (Isaiah 42:8). Be blessed!
